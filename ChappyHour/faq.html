What is the dress code?

The dress code is Formal. We suggest that men wear suits of any color (no shorts, please!) and women wear mid to floor-length dresses. Due to the location being on a golf course, we suggest block or wedge heels instead of stilettos to avoid sinking into the grass.

What is the weather like?

In August on Martha's Vineyard, average high temperatures range from the upper 70s to low 80s Fahrenheit (around 25-28 degrees Celsius), and average low temperatures usually fall in the 60s Fahrenheit (around 15-20 degrees Celsius).
